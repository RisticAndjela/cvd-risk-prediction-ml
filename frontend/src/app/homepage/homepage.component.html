Cardiovascular Risk Awareness

What is cardiovascular risk?

Cardiovascular risk represents the probability of developing heart or blood vessel diseases, such as heart attack, stroke, or hypertension, based on lifestyle and health indicators.

Cardiovascular diseases (CVD) are a group of disorders of the heart and blood vessels. They include conditions such as coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmias, and cerebrovascular disease (stroke). CVD is the leading cause of death globally, but many cases can be prevented with early detection and lifestyle changes.

Risk factors include high blood pressure, high cholesterol, smoking, diabetes, obesity, physical inactivity, and an unhealthy diet. Genetic factors and age also play a role.

How this app helps

Our tool uses machine learning trained on medical data to estimate the likelihood of cardiovascular disease. It analyzes factors such as:

  • Age, gender, height, and weight
  • Blood pressure (systolic and diastolic)
  • Cholesterol and glucose levels
  • Smoking, alcohol use, and physical activity

Important note

This app does not replace medical consultation. Its purpose is to support awareness and early prevention, not to provide a diagnosis. If your result indicates higher risk, please consult your doctor for a full examination.

Take control of your health

Even small changes — balanced diet, regular exercise, quitting smoking — can dramatically reduce your risk. Start by checking your results and taking the first step toward a healthier heart.
